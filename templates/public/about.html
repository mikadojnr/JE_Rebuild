Our Story

Founded with a vision to redefine financial excellence in Nigeria, John & Eniola Consultancy has grown from a small advisory firm to one of the most trusted names in financial services across West Africa.

We recognized the need for comprehensive, strategic financial solutions that go beyond traditional accounting services. We bridge the gap between complex financial regulations and practical business needs.

Today, we proudly serve clients across diverse industries, managing assets worth over ₦2 billion and maintaining a 98% client satisfaction rate. Our success is built on an unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation.
